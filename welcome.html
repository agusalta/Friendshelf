Welcome to Friendshelf

Thank you for installing Friendshelf! This extension will help you find the best software deals and reviews directly in your browser.

How to Use Friendshelf

  • Click on the extension icon to open the pop up.
  • Search for products in google and get detailed information on deals and reviews.
  • Use the dark mode switch for a better visual experience.

Main Features

  • Highlighting text on web pages with relevant product information.
  • Displaying product data including ratings, reviews, and pricing.
  • Quick access to updated deals and pricing plans.
